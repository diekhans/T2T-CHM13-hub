Description

BLAT of RefSeq currated RNAs (NM_ and NR_) to T2T CHM13 assembly

Methods

Methods are based on those used by the UCSC browser.

  1. Obtained GRCh38 RefSeq RNA dated 2020-05-29 from NCBI.
  2. Aligned to T2t CHM13 using blat -q=rna -fine -minScore=20 -stepSize=5 -noHead.
  3. Estimate Poly(A) lengths with faPolyASizes.
  4. Filter resulting alignment with pslCDnaFilter -minQSize=20 -ignoreIntrons -repsAsMatch -ignoreNs -bestOverlap -polyASizes=<polya> -localNearBest=0.001 -minId=0.92 -minCover=0.75
