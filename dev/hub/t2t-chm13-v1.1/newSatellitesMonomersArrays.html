Description

New Satellite track for CHM13v1.1

All new satellite loci are shown as a single block: start-end of the entire array or lone monomer

Methods

Identification and annotation of new satellites was accomplished through a combination of RepeatModeler2.01, RepeatMasker(p4.1.2-p1) and manual curation as described in Hoyt et al. 2021
